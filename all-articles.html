The Future of Education has arrived

27 September Christina Baumann Curriculum, Education

One of the problems with mainstream education is that it relies on students learning at the same pace, in the same way. Most schools do not accommodate the fact that some students need extra attention whereas others flourish when given more leeway. They also don’t allow for different teaching and learning methods, which means that some students "slip through the cracks" when they don’t respond to a conventional approach.

Study Tips backed by Science

17 August Author Name Studying 0 Comments

Change it up! Spending hours and hours in the library may ease your conscience (and subdue your parents) but studies suggest that changing your studying environment can help you retain information more effectively. Your brain tends to associate the same material with different locales, forcing the memory to strengthen with a variety of different associations.
